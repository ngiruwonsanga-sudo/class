ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

UR-CE, RUKARA Campus, is an all-inclusive higher learning institution which only focuses on individual's entry qualification of two principle passes. Students from the main stream education system who successfully complete Advanced Level of Education are admitted depending on their profile. Detailed information on the requirements is communicated officially every year.
